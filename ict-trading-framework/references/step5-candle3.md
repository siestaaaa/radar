# Step 5: Candle 3

## Candle Numbering

| Candle | Role |
|--------|------|
| Candle 1 | Before swing |
| Candle 2 | Swing point (high/low) |
| Candle 3 | After swing (confirmation) |
| Candle 4 | Expansion |

## Closure Types

### Continuation Closure
- Price sweeps previous high/low
- Closes BEYOND it
- Trend likely continues

### Reversal Closure
- Price sweeps previous high/low
- Closes BACK INSIDE
- Potential reversal

## Candle 3 Closure

When Candle 2 fails to give closure:

- Bullish: Candle 3 closes OVER body of Candle 2
- Bearish: Candle 3 closes BELOW body of Candle 2
- Validates delayed confirmation
- Anticipate Candle 4 as expansion

## Quantified Rules

- Candle 3 body >= 40% of total range
- Close over/under C2 body by measurable amount
- Equilibrium wick >= 30% in correct half
- Displacement within 2 candles after C3

## Equilibrium Logic

1. Mark full range of Candle 3 (high to low)
2. Calculate 50% level
3. Bullish: C4 wick into UPPER half (50-100%)
4. Bearish: C4 wick into LOWER half (0-50%)
5. Entry within designated half

## Decision Logic

1. Check C2: valid closure?
2. If no → wait for C3
3. Check C3: close beyond C2 body?
4. Mark C3 range
5. Calculate equilibrium
6. Wait C4: wick into correct half?
7. Drop LTF: displacement within equilibrium?
8. If all true → entry valid

## Connection to Previous Steps

## Works When

- C3 body >= 40% (not doji)
- Clear close over/under C2
- Equilibrium clear
- Displacement within 2 candles
- All TFs aligned

## Fails When

- C3 body < 30% (indecision)
- C3 within C2 body (no closure)
- Equilibrium violated
- No displacement within 3 candles
- Protected swing broken

## No Trade Conditions

- C2 AND C3 lack closure
- C3 range < 0.5 ATR
- Equilibrium unclear
- No displacement after C3
- Conflicting HTF bias

## Note

- C3 = timing confirmation
- C3 = backup when C2 fails
- Pair with Point of Interest
- Wait for LTF displacement
