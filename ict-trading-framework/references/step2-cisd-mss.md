# Step 2: CISD & MSS

## Definitions

### CISD (Change in State of Delivery)
- Price closes beyond the OPEN of last up-close (bearish) or down-close (bullish) candle
- Confirms shift in order flow
- Often appears earlier than MSS

### MSS (Market Structure Shift)
- Price displaces and closes beyond swing high or swing low
- Confirms trend change at structural level
- Later but safer signal

## Key Rules

- CISD confirms shift in order flow (not limited to order blocks)
- MSS confirms broader trend change
- CISD + MSS together = highest probability
- CISD alone = earlier but riskier
- MSS alone = later but safer

## Liquidity Condition

- CISD/MSS VALID only AFTER liquidity interaction
- If no liquidity interaction → low probability
- Always define: from WHICH liquidity → to WHICH liquidity

## Mode Logic

### REVERSAL
1. Liquidity sweep
2. CISD forms
3. MSS confirms
4. Move to opposite liquidity

### CONTINUATION
1. Pullback
2. CISD in trend direction
3. Continue to next liquidity

## Displacement Rules

- Strong candle body (dominant close)
- Close beyond level (not wick)
- Creates imbalance (FVG)
- Momentum: consecutive candles same direction

## Invalidation

- Wick-only break → ignore
- Price returns inside range after MSS → false shift
- Opposite strong displacement → invalidate bias
- No follow-through after CISD → weak signal

## Confirmation Logic

- Check for ANY valid confirmation (CISD and/or MSS)
- CISD = early signal
- MSS = confirmed signal
- Both together = highest probability

## Entry Readiness

- Liquidity interaction confirmed
- Valid displacement present
- At least one confirmation (CISD or MSS)
- Bias aligns with Step 1 target
- If all true → proceed to Step 3
- If not → WAIT

## Time Alignment

- Prefer confirmations during active sessions
- Avoid signals during low volatility
- Session opens increase valid displacement probability

## No Trade Conditions

- No liquidity interaction
- No displacement
- No CISD or MSS
- Conflicting signals (bullish vs bearish)
- Unclear mode (reversal vs continuation)

## Connection to Step 1

- Step 1: WHERE price wants to go
- Step 2: IF price is actually moving there
- CISD/MSS validates ONLY after liquidity interaction
- Mode must align with Step 1 target
