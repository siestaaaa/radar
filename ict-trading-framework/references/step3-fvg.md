# Step 3: Fair Value Gap (FVG)

## Definition

FVG = 3 candle pattern showing price inefficiency

### Bullish FVG (BISI)
- Low of candle 1 does NOT overlap with high of candle 3
- Buy side imbalance, sell side inefficiency

### Bearish FVG (SIBI)
- High of candle 1 does NOT overlap with low of candle 3
- Sell side imbalance, buy side inefficiency

## Key Rules

- No overlap = imbalance
- Overlap = efficiency (not FVG)
- Consequent encroachment (CE) = 50% level of FVG
- Entry zone = within FVG boundaries
- Refined entry = at or near CE (50%)

## FVG Invalidation Refined

- Close through FULL FVG = invalid
- Close slightly beyond CE = still usable (context dependent)
- Strong displacement through FVG = invalid
- Respect with rejection = still valid

## FVG Quality Filter

- Clear displacement candle (large body, minimal wick)
- FVG not inside heavy consolidation
- FVG has clean price path
- Prefer fresh FVG (first touch)
- Avoid overlapping multiple messy FVGs

## Entry Model Refinement

| Type | Entry Point | Risk |
|------|-------------|------|
| Aggressive | Touch FVG | Higher |
| Refined | CE (50%) | Medium |
| Conservative | LTF confirmation | Lower |

## Decision Logic

1. After Step 2, identify FVG created by displacement
2. Mark FVG zone (candle 1 to candle 3)
3. Mark CE (50%) as optimal entry
4. Wait for price to retrace into FVG
5. Entry based on model
6. Target = Step 1 liquidity
7. Stop = opposite side of FVG

## Entry Readiness

- Step 2 confirmation present
- Valid FVG from displacement
- FVG passes quality filter
- FVG NOT fully broken
- Price retracing into zone

## Connection to Step 2

- Step 2 creates FVG via displacement
- FVG is RESULT of confirmation candle
- Without Step 2 → no valid FVG
- Entry zone aligns with Step 2 direction

## Works When

- FVG from strong displacement
- FVG passes quality filter
- Price retraces to CE or FVG zone
- FVG aligns with Step 1 direction
- First touch preferred

## Fails When

- FVG fully broken
- FVG tested multiple times
- FVG in heavy consolidation
- No Step 2 confirmation
- Trading into FVG against target

## No Trade Conditions

- FVG fully broken
- Price left FVG zone
- No quality FVG from displacement
- FVG conflicts with target
- Opposite displacement after FVG

## Note

- FVG is ENTRY ZONE, not trigger
- Trigger comes from Step 2
- FVG defines WHERE, not WHEN
- CE is reference, not death line
